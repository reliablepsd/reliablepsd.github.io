OVERVIEW

CAR-T: cutting edge therapies require a cutting edge solution

CARs are articial antigen receptors proteins, composed of fragments, or subdomains, of naturally occurring and articial entities. For scientists working on these therapies, the complex CAR proteins create challenges for laboratory data capture, management and utilization. Accurately dening the proteins and capturing their full description is critical for robust intellectual property (IP) protection. Tracking relationships between variant candidate entities is also essential. Supporting the complex laboratory workows used to create the fragments and whole proteins requires both exibility and standardization. And once data about the candidates are available, scientists need to see it all, and in real time, to make well-informed decisions.

Do you need to:

Protect Your Intellectual Property?

CARs are novel biological entities, designed by biologists to provide the ideal combination of antigen-binding and cellular response. These complex entities, devised through advanced understanding and application of leading-edge science, need an equally innovative informatics solution. The Dotmatics solution allows scientists to fully define and capture this proprietary information, ensuring robust IP protection for both the CARs and the processes used to engineer them.

Track and Secure Your Data?

A CAR research project will result in many candidate molecules derived from a variety of precursor proteins. Tracking the relationships between the precursors, CAR subdomains, and the full CARs is essential for a successful project. The Dotmatics solution supports full traceability of all entities used and generated within the project and allows users to easily see and track those relationships.

Flexible and agile data capture?

Development of the CARs requires iterations of experiment development and execution. As the project evolves, experiments transition from primarily ad hoc to standardized workflows. The Dotmatics solution provides flexible and agile experiment data capture capability that always meet the needs of the scientists. It ensures complete data capture to provide high quality data and accelerate future CAR projects.

BENEFITS

Here to support you

Track and Secure Your Data

Dotmatics Bioregister, our application for dening and registering biological molecules tracks all relationships across full CAR proteins and all subdomains that comprise the molecules. Uniqueness checking includes chemical descriptions to provide IP protection.

Capture, Share, Repeat

Dotmatics Studies Notebook, our electronic laboratory notebook (ELN), manages experi-mental workows. The ELN supports the development of the full-length CAR protein, as well as its constituent subdomains. Studies Notebook is exible, supporting both ad hoc experiments and structured workows.

Visualize to Analyze

Dotmatics Browser provides a comprehensive view of project data enabling scientists tosearch, review and interpret information about their CAR candidates. Data views are congurable and interactive, supporting both individual investigation and collaboration.

Browser

Dotmatics Browser is a powerful web-based search and reporting solution that provides federated searching across Dotmatics and third party research databases, enabling research scientists to access all of their data whenever they need it, irrespective of where it is stored.

  • Integrates chemical, biological, assay, notebook and other research databases to provide a single federated search and browse capability
  • Provides scientific search, such as chemical substructure and similarity
  • Form-based querying and browsing – project-level and user level forms designed using drag and drop
