We’re Makeena:
a loyalty app for good

We want everyone on this big, beautiful planet of ours to easily ﬁnd and afford healthier food, eco-friendly products, you know, the good stuf. So we created an app that instantly connects you to better choices — products that are better for your health, better for your life, and oh yeah, better for the planet.

Like many of the products you’ll ﬁnd on our app, Makeena is part of a larger movement — a movement to build better businesses. Being B-Certiﬁed means we’re socially responsible and committed to BEING THE CHANGE we wish to see in the world. We give back to environmental nonproﬁts as a member of 1% for the Planet and partner with other mindful companies.

We believe small choices impact the world big time.

We chose the name Makeena because it means happiness in Swahili and abundance in Hawaiian. We like to say Makeena also means empowerment because it puts the power of shopping for good directly into the hands of big-hearted consumers like you.
