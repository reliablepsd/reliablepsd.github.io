The Preacher’s Corner

Since its founding, The Order of Preachers has taken the joyful message of the Gospel wherever it was needed — in churches, in schools, and in town squares.

It is in honor of this legacy that the Dominican House of Studies has created the Preacher’s Corner, a place to find engaging, thought-provoking, and spiritually-nourishing content from Dominican Friars at the DHS.

This growing media library was created in gratitude for the generous souls on whom the DHS relies to forward our vital mission.
