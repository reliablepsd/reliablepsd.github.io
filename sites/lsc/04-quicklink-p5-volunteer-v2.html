How to become a Legacy volunteer

To volunteer at The Legacy Senior Communities, each individual or group leader must complete the volunteer application. Volunteers will receive an email with information on the volunteer onboarding process, which includes a background check. Once approved, you will be notified of the volunteer positions that are available.
