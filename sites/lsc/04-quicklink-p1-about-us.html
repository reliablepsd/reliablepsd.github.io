Foundation

Leave a lasting legacy

Since opening in 1953, The Legacy Senior Communities has been dedicated to providing housing and health care services to seniors. By making a gift, you ensure that our seniors receive the highest quality care and services. The Legacy Senior Community Foundation supports our efforts to provide financial assistance to those who need it, allowing seniors to live with comfort and dignity.
