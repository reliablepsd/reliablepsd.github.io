MIDTOWN PARK

Services & Amenities

Vibrant, Inspired and Secure

Enjoy a carefree, active retirement lifestyle enhanced with an array of upscale amenities and services. Plus, through the comprehensive Life Care plan, care is available onsite should you ever need it.

DOWNLOAD PDF

Community Amenities

  • Restaurant-style dining: Tamarisk Restaurant, Stanley’s Bistro, and Michael’s Pub
  • Fitness center and spa featuring state-of-the-art equipment, aerobics studio, and heated indoor pool with lap and resistance areas
  • Full-service salon and barbershop
  • Sanctuar
  • Library, art studio, and card rooms
  • Theater
  • Weekly happy hour
  • Full-time lifestyle director and fitness director
  • Dog park
  • Bocce ball court
  • Walking path
  • Sculpture gardens
  • Storage units
  • Secured underground parking
  • Pet-friendly campus

Apartment Home Amenities

  • Walk-in closets
  • Washer/dryer units
  • Fully equipped kitchens
  • Weekly housekeeping
  • Individual climate controls
  • 24-hour emergence alert system

Services

  • Weekly housekeeping
  • Flat linen service
  • Interior and exterior maintenance
  • Personal and group transportation
  • Fine and casual dining
  • Wellness training, fitness classes, and aqua aerobics
  • Social and cultural activities and events
  • Religious programming
  • Complimentary breakfast
  • Wi-Fi hotspots
  • Gated community with 21-hour security
  • Dry-cleaning pickup/delivery
  • Catering for private events
  • Massage and spa treatments
  • Onsite primary care physician
