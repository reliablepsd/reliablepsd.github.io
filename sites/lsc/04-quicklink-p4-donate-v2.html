PLANNED GIVING

Leave a Legacy Society

The Leave a Legacy Society is composed of thoughtful, forward-thinking donors like you who have opted to make their values known to future generations through one of the many different types of planned gifts. Leave a Legacy Society members will be recognized in perpetuity in printed materials and on signage at The Legacy Willow Bend and The Legacy Midtown Park campuses.

Learn more

GIVING

Financial Assistance Endowment Fund

These funds further our commitment to our seniors, making our services available to those who need financial help in order to pay for care. Partnering with the Jewish community and the entire Legacy community, we plan to grow our endowment to ensure we have the resources available to assist those in need.

  • Legacy Willow Bend: The Provides long-term Medicaid rooms in the Healthcare Center for eligible low-income seniors and; Assisted Living and Memory Support rental subsidies for eligible seniors.
  • Legacy at Home Personal Assistance Services:The Services decrease the risk of injury and/or hospitalization for low-income seniors still living at home by providing assistance with bathing, grooming and dressing, meal preparation, light housekeeping and laundry, escort to doctors’ visits, shopping, errands, companion care, recovery from illness, and transitioning home from hospitalization.
  • The Legacy Midtown Park: Provides long-term Medicaid rooms in the Healthcare Center for eligible, low-income seniors and; rental subsidies for eligible seniors.
GIVING

Join the Leadership Giving Circle

We are so grateful for the generous support of our Leadership Giving Circle. Join these charitable donors by making an annual gift of $1,000 or more to support annual operations, our Financial Assistance Fund, Grateful Hearts, or Jewish Life. These funds allow LSC to sustain the quality and compassionate care that we have provided the Jewish community for more than 65 years.-
