SERvices & AMENITIES

Everything You Need (and Want)

Located in West Plano, life at The Legacy Willow Bend offers beautiful surroundings and resort-style services and amenities. Residents can visit with one another while getting fit in the Aaron Family Spa and Fitness Center or eat in one of our delicious dining options.

We offer a variety of ways for residents to stay active and independent, including visits to local attractions, interesting guest speakers, themed happy hours and many other opportunities to enjoy day-to-day life. We’re pet friendly too!

LIFE CARE

Live in Security

The Legacy Willow Bend is a Life Care Retirement Community which combines upscale senior living in a resort-style setting with the best quality healthcare options for your present or future healthcare needs.

Our Life Care program not only ensures you’ll be able to take advantage of all our community offers, it also gives you the security of priority access to Assisted Living, Memory Care, Short-Term Rehabilitation and Long-Term Care. Life Care ensures that you will always have predictably low healthcare costs.
